Server Hosting Costs

Running a high-performance Minecraft server requires dedicated hardware to ensure a lag-free experience for everyone. Monthly root server bills, extensive DDoS protection, automated off-site backups, and web hosting domains cost a significant amount of money.

Development & Maintenance

Maintaining custom plugins, keeping the Modpack updated, optimizing server performance, and fixing bugs takes hundreds of hours of voluntary work. Your support helps to keep the development alive and guarantees that we can continue bringing new, exciting features to the Craftify community without interruptions.

Future Roadmap & Goals

We have massive plans for Craftify. With your continued support, our next major milestones include upgrading our primary server node for increased player slots, commissioning exclusive custom minigames, expanding our proprietary modpack with unique content, and hosting larger community events with real, tangible prizes. Every contribution brings us one step closer to making Craftify the ultimate Minecraft experience.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I get in-game advantages for donating?

To comply strictly with the Minecraft EULA, we do not offer any "pay-to-win" advantages. Supporters may receive cosmetic perks, special Discord roles, or early access to new non-competitive features, but core gameplay remains fair and balanced for everyone.

Are donations refundable?

Since funds are immediately put towards server costs and active development, donations are generally non-refundable. Please only support us if you have the financial means to do so comfortably. If there is a processing error, please contact our support team on Discord.

Where exactly does the money go?

100% of the revenue is reinvested into the project. This covers the monthly root server rental, high-speed web hosting, domain renewals, premium plugin licenses, enhanced DDoS protection infrastructure, and occasionally commissioning external developers for complex custom features.

How else can I help?

We rely heavily on the generosity of our community and ad revenue to keep the lights on. Whitelisting our site on your AdBlocker is a completely free and incredibly easy way to support us while you are browsing our updates and news. Engaging with our community, voting for the server on tracking lists, and inviting your friends are also fantastic ways to help us grow!
