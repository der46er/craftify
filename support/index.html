Transparency: Where does the money go?

We believe in complete transparency. Running a server of this scale is not cheap. Here is exactly what your donations and ad impressions help fund:

  • Dedicated Root Servers High clock-speed CPUs and large amounts of NVMe RAM are required to keep heavily modded servers lag-free.
  • DDoS Protection & Backups Enterprise-grade network filtering to prevent malicious attacks, plus automated off-site cloud backups to ensure world data is never lost.
  • Premium Plugins & Development Licenses for professional anti-cheat systems, custom plugin commissions, web hosting, domain renewals, and asset creation.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I get in-game advantages for donating?

To comply strictly with the Minecraft EULA, we do not offer any "pay-to-win" advantages. Supporters may receive cosmetic perks, special Discord roles, or early access to new non-competitive features, but core gameplay remains fair and balanced for everyone.

Are donations refundable?

Since funds are immediately put towards server costs and active development, donations are generally non-refundable. Please only support us if you have the financial means to do so comfortably. If there is a processing error, please contact our support team on Discord.

Other ways to help!

We rely heavily on the generosity of our community and ad revenue to keep the lights on. Whitelisting our site on your AdBlocker is a completely free and incredibly easy way to support us while you are browsing our updates and news. Engaging with our community, voting for the server on tracking lists, and simply playing and inviting your friends are also fantastic ways to help us grow!
