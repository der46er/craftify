Server Hosting Costs

Running a high-performance Minecraft server requires dedicated hardware to ensure a lag-free experience for everyone. Monthly root server bills, DDoS protection, and backups cost a significant amount of money. By simply viewing the ads on this page, you directly contribute to keeping the server online.

Development & Maintenance

Maintaining custom plugins, keeping the Modpack updated, and fixing bugs takes hundreds of hours of voluntary work. Your support helps to keep the development alive and guarantees that we can continue bringing new, exciting features to the Craftify community.

Community Events

We regularly host tournaments, events, and giveaways. The generated revenue allows us to give back to the community by offering better prizes, upgrading our infrastructure for large-scale events, and rewarding active players.
