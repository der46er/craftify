The TrainCarts Update is here XD
Here is what you can do now:

**Speed Control**
You can now adjust the max speed of your minecarts
/train speedlimit <number>

**Claim Trains**
Protect your minecarts from being messed with
/train claim

**Destroy Trains**
Remove stuck carts
/train destroy

**Rename Trains**
Give your trains custom names
/train name <name>

**Sign Building**
Place a sign directly under the track and write:
Line 1: [train]
Line 2: Action (e.g., property)
Line 3: Setting (e.g., speedlimit)
Line 4: Value (e.g., 2.0)
As soon as a minecart passes over it, the effect is automatically applied to the train.
